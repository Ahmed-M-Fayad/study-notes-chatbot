# Study Notes Chatbot

A personal AI assistant that answers questions from your lecture notes and course materials.

## What is this?

This chatbot reads your study materials (PDFs, lecture notes, textbooks) and answers questions based on their content. Instead of manually searching through documents, simply ask your question and get instant answers with source citations.

## How it works

1. Upload your study materials (PDF files)
2. The bot processes and organizes the content
3. Ask any question about your materials
4. Get accurate answers with references to source documents

## Example Questions

**Computer Science:**
- "What is the difference between supervised and unsupervised learning?"
- "Explain how merge sort works"
- "What are the ACID properties in databases?"

**Mathematics:**
- "What is the Central Limit Theorem?"
- "How do you calculate standard deviation?"

**General:**
- "Summarize the main points about [topic]"
- "What did the lecture say about [concept]?"
- "List the key terms from [chapter]"

## Key Features

- Answer questions from multiple documents
- Show which document contains the answer
- Works with all your course materials in one place
- Fast information retrieval without manual searching

## Use Cases

**Exam Preparation:** Quickly review specific topics and test understanding

**Assignment Help:** Find relevant information and verify concepts

**Study Sessions:** Create personalized Q&A guides from your materials

## Example Interaction

```
Question: "How many layers does a neural network have?"
```
